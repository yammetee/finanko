Privacy Policy

Effective: 11 July 2026
Finanko handles personal financial information. This notice explains what is collected, why it is used, which service providers receive it, and the choices available to you.

1. Controller and contact

Finanko is operated as the Finanko project (“Finanko”, “we”, “us”). For privacy requests, account deletion, access, correction, or portability, contact a.luganko@gmail.com.

2. Data we process

  • Account data: email address, authentication identifiers, session information, and optional account metadata.
  • Financial data you provide: portfolio names, accounts, opening balances, currencies, categories, transactions, recurring rules, debts, interest rates, descriptions, and receipt line items.
  • Receipt and AI inputs: receipt images, expense text, selected category names, and compact portfolio aggregates submitted when you request AI parsing or analysis.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser/device information, timestamps, request metadata, security logs, and error information generated by hosting and infrastructure providers.
  • Exchange-rate requests: your browser requests current rates from the configured exchange-rate provider; that provider may receive normal network metadata such as IP address.

3. Purposes and legal bases

We process account and financial data to provide the service you request, authenticate you, synchronize records, calculate balances and analytics, parse receipts, and return requested AI analysis. The principal legal basis is performance of the Terms of Use and steps requested before entering that contract. Security, fraud prevention, reliability, and limited operational logging rely on legitimate interests. Where applicable law requires consent for an optional feature, we will request it separately.

4. AI processing

AI features are optional and run only when you request them. Receipt parsing sends the selected image and necessary category context to OpenAI through a server-side Vercel function. Portfolio analysis sends compact aggregates rather than raw multi-year transaction history. AI output may be incorrect and is not financial, investment, tax, legal, or credit advice. Finanko does not use AI output to make decisions that produce legal or similarly significant effects about you.

5. Service providers and disclosures

  • Supabase: authentication and relational database hosting.
  • Vercel: web hosting and serverless API execution.
  • OpenAI: receipt parsing and portfolio analysis when invoked.
  • Exchange-rate provider: current currency-rate retrieval.

We do not sell personal data, run behavioural advertising, or disclose financial records to data brokers. Data may also be disclosed when required by law, to protect users or the service, or in connection with a lawful business transfer.

6. International transfers

Providers may process data in countries outside your residence. Where required, transfers are handled using legally recognised safeguards made available by the relevant provider, such as adequacy decisions or standard contractual clauses. Review the providers’ current privacy and data-transfer documentation for their specific locations and safeguards.

7. Storage, cookies, and local storage

Finanko does not currently use advertising or analytics cookies. Supabase Auth uses browser local storage or equivalent strictly necessary client storage to maintain your signed-in session. Language preference is not persistently stored. No optional cookie banner is shown because no optional cookies are currently set by Finanko. If optional analytics or marketing technologies are introduced, consent controls will be added before they are activated where required.

8. Retention

Account and financial data are retained while your account remains active or until deletion is requested, unless a longer period is necessary for security, dispute resolution, or legal obligations. Provider backups and logs may remain for limited provider-controlled retention periods before deletion cycles complete. Receipt images are transmitted for parsing and are not stored in the Finanko relational database; parsed transaction data is stored when you save it.

9. Security

Finanko uses authenticated access, owner-scoped row-level security, encrypted HTTPS transport, server-side AI credentials, and access controls. No online service can guarantee absolute security. Use a unique password and protect access to your email and device.

10. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may request access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection, or a portable copy of your personal data, and may complain to your local data-protection authority. We may verify your identity before fulfilling a request. Contact the address above. Requests are handled within the period required by applicable law.

11. Children

Finanko is not directed to children under 16 and should not be used by anyone unable to enter the Terms under applicable law. Contact us if you believe a child provided personal data.

12. Changes

We may update this notice when features, providers, or legal requirements change. Material changes will be communicated in the service or during sign-in where appropriate. The effective date above identifies the current version.
